CORPORATE REPUTATION & TRANSFORMATION

Businesses today operate in a VUCA world, the one witnessing intense scrutiny from media, investors, activists and regulators. At the same time, there are seismic forces surrounding them – be it technology or security, cultural or political shifts, and much more.

Ruder Finn India helps position companies for success in this fast-changing world by building reputation and admiration among external stakeholders and the public, as well as deep emotional engagement among employees and partners.

Our unique combination of domain knowledge and communication expertise enables us to drive storytelling in a unique way. With a corporate team consisting of former journalists, MBAs, bankers, lawyers, integrated marketing professionals, we’re able to bisect our client’s business, strategy and context for communication and drive the narrative without any misalignment of the messaging across platforms and stakeholders.

With our own and network offices across India and our dual Headquarters in China and the US, we’re able to ensure proximity to the influencers that matter the most besides pre-empting risks for our communication programme.
